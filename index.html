About Anshu Pustak Pashal

Established in the heart of Golbazar, Asanpur, Siraha, Anshu Pustak Pashal has been serving the educational needs of students and the literary interests of the community for years. Our shop is more than just a bookstore - it's a hub for learning, discovery, and community engagement.

Located at Q8PH+GJ2 / Q8PH+GHP, Golbazar 56500, we pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of textbooks, solution guides, stationery, and local publications. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find exactly what you need.

We're particularly proud to support local authors by featuring their works and hosting book launch events, making us not just a commercial establishment but a cultural center for the community.
